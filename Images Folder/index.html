LIGHTNING

Thunder is the sound caused by lightning. It can be heard up to 10 miles away from the lightning strike.

  1. Lightning is a sudden electrostatic discharge that occurs during a thunderstorm.
  2. It is caused by the buildup of electrical charges in clouds.
  3. Lightning can heat the air around it to temperatures five times hotter than the sun's surface.
  4. The rapid expansion of heated air causes the sound we know as thunder.
  5. Lightning strikes the Earth about 8 million times per day.
